Privacy Policy

Vrefos (com.imaliouris.vrefos) · Effective July 6, 2026

The short version: everything you record in Vrefos stays on your device. The app collects nothing, transmits nothing, and shares or sells nothing. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no ads.

Vrefos is a baby activity tracker for parents and caregivers. It runs entirely on your device: it has no server component, requires no sign-up, and works without an internet connection. This policy explains what information the app stores locally and why it requests the permissions it does.

Information you enter

To do its job, Vrefos stores the information you type into it:

  • Your children's names and (optionally) birth dates.
  • Activity records — feeding and sleep times, food types, and related details.
  • Your reminder and notification preferences.

All of this is saved only in a local database on your device. It is never uploaded, synced, backed up to our systems, or made accessible to us or anyone else.

Information we collect

None. Vrefos has no server and makes no network requests to us or to any third party. We have no analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking of any kind, and no third-party services receive data from the app.

App permissions

Vrefos requests a small set of Android permissions, all of which support its on-device reminders:

Permission Why the app uses it
Notifications To show the feeding and sleep reminders you set up.
Exact alarms So reminders fire at the exact time you chose rather than approximately.
Run after restart To re-register your scheduled reminders after the device reboots, since Android clears pending alarms on restart.
Vibration & wake lock Standard notification delivery — vibrating and briefly waking the device when a reminder fires.
Internet & network state Used solely by the app's embedded local runtime, which serves the app's interface from within the device itself. No data is transmitted off the device.

Children's privacy

Vrefos is designed for and directed at parents and caregivers, not at children. The information you enter about your children stays on your device, under your control, and is never transmitted anywhere.

Data retention and deletion

Your data exists only on your device, so you are always in full control of it:

  • Delete individual records or children at any time inside the app.
  • Remove everything by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

There are no remote copies for us to delete — once you remove the data from your device, it is gone.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Vrefos changes how it handles data — for example, by adding an optional sync or backup feature — this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the effective date above will change.
